⚡ Quick Setup Guide — Connect Firebase & EmailJS

STEP 1 — Firebase

Edit the FIREBASE_CONFIG block in app.js with your project credentials from console.firebase.google.com

STEP 2 — EmailJS

Sign up at emailjs.com → Create a Gmail service → Create a template with variables: {{case_name}}, {{court}}, {{date}}, {{to_email}}

STEP 3 — Reminders

Update EMAILJS_CONFIG with your Public Key, Service ID, and Template ID from your EmailJS dashboard.
